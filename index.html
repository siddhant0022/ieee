ABOUT CONFERENCE
The International Conference on Computing, Communication, and Sustainable Energy Technologies ICEC2SET-2025) shall serve as a vital platform for researchers, academics, and industry professionals to converge and exchange cutting-edge advancements. It will foster a dynamic atmosphere where the synergy between computing, communication, and sustainable energy is explored to the required depth. Participants delve into topics ranging from artificial intelligence, data science, and machine learning applications in energy management to the development of robust communication networks, wireless power transmission. Discussions encompass the latest breakthroughs in renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, and the integration of sustainable practices into technological frameworks along with IoT, Edge Computing..etc. The conference emphasizes the critical role of innovations in addressing global energy challenges while promoting environmentally conscious solutions. Through keynote speeches, technical sessions, and poster presentations, the conference facilitates knowledge dissemination and collaboration, aiming to drive the development of sustainable and technologically advanced solutions for a better future. Further details shall be provided in conference tracks.
